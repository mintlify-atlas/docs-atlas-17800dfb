Learn how to create and configure a loyalty campaign on Stampchain. This guide covers all the form fields and configuration options available when setting up your blockchain-powered loyalty program.

## Overview

Stampchain campaigns allow you to create blockchain-based loyalty programs where customers can collect tokens by scanning QR codes. Each campaign has its own token economics, distribution rules, and expiration settings.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Campaign Creation">
    From your admin dashboard, click the "New Campaign" button or navigate to `/admin/campaign/new` to access the campaign creation form.
    
    The campaign creation interface is divided into three main sections: Campaign Details, Token Configuration, and Security & Limits.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Campaign Details">
    The first section collects basic information about your loyalty program:

    ### Campaign Name (Required)
    Enter a descriptive name for your campaign, such as "Coffee Shop Loyalty Program" or "YouTube Subscriber Rewards". This name will be visible to users when they claim tokens.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/campaign-form.tsx:31`

    ### Token Symbol (Required)
    Create a short symbol for your tokens (maximum 10 characters). For example:
    - `COFFEE` for a coffee shop
    - `YT` for YouTube rewards
    - `STAMP` for a general loyalty program

    The symbol is automatically converted to uppercase.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/campaign-form.tsx:41`

    ### Token Image (Required)
    Upload an image that represents your token. This will be displayed to users during the claim process. Accepted formats include PNG, JPG, and other standard image formats.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/campaign-form.tsx:54`

    ### Description (Optional)
    Provide a description of your loyalty program. Explain what tokens can be used for and any special benefits. This helps users understand the value proposition of participating in your program.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/campaign-form.tsx:67`

    <Note>
      Screenshot placeholder: Campaign Details form with all fields filled out
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Token Configuration">
    Configure the token economics for your campaign:

    ### Total Token Supply (Required)
    Set the maximum number of tokens that will be created for this campaign. For example, `10000` tokens. Once this supply is exhausted, no more tokens can be distributed.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/token-form.tsx:31`

    ### Tokens per Claim (Required)
    Specify how many tokens users receive each time they scan a QR code. Common values are `5`, `10`, or `25` tokens per claim.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/token-form.tsx:42`

    ### Max Claims per Wallet (Optional)
    Limit how many times a single wallet can claim tokens. This helps prevent abuse. For example, setting this to `5` means each user can claim tokens up to 5 times. Leave empty for unlimited claims.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/token-form.tsx:53`

    ### Blockchain Network (Required)
    Select the blockchain where your tokens will be deployed:
    - **Solana (SPL)**: Fast and low-cost transactions
    - **Base (ERC-20)**: Ethereum Layer 2 network

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/token-form.tsx:64`

    <Note>
      Screenshot placeholder: Token Configuration section showing all fields
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Security & Limits">
    Set up fraud prevention and campaign expiration:

    ### Campaign Expiration (Optional)
    Toggle the "Campaign Expiration" switch to enable a time limit for your campaign. When enabled, users will not be able to claim tokens after the specified date.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/expiry-form.tsx:34`

    ### Expiration Date
    If expiration is enabled, select the date when the campaign should end. After this date, QR codes will no longer be valid for claiming tokens.

    **Location in code:** `src/app/(protected-route)/admin/campaign/new/components/expiry-form.tsx:43`

    <Warning>
      Once a campaign expires, users cannot claim tokens even if QR codes are still available. Plan your expiration date carefully.
    </Warning>

    <Note>
      Screenshot placeholder: Security & Limits section with expiration enabled
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Preview and Submit">
    Before submitting, review your campaign configuration in the preview panel on the right side of the screen. This shows how your campaign will appear to users.

    Once you're satisfied with all settings, click the "Create Campaign" button to deploy your tokens to the blockchain and create your campaign.

    <Info>
      Campaign creation involves deploying a smart contract to the blockchain, which may take a few moments. You'll be redirected to the success page once the deployment is complete.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Campaign Created Successfully">
    After successful creation, you'll be redirected to the campaign success page where you can:
    - View your campaign summary
    - Generate QR codes for token distribution
    - Access campaign management tools

    See the [Generating QR Codes](/guides/generating-qr-codes) guide for next steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Token Supply Planning" icon="calculator">
    Calculate your total supply based on expected participants and claims per user. For example: 200 users × 5 claims × 10 tokens = 10,000 total supply.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Meaningful Symbols" icon="tag">
    Choose token symbols that are memorable and related to your brand. Short, uppercase symbols work best (e.g., COFFEE, SHOP, REWARDS).
  </Card>

  <Card title="Clear Descriptions" icon="file-text">
    Write descriptions that clearly explain the benefits and use cases for your tokens. This increases user engagement and participation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Expiration Strategy" icon="clock">
    Use expiration dates for time-limited promotions or seasonal campaigns. For ongoing programs, leave expiration disabled.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Example Campaign Configurations

### Coffee Shop Loyalty Program
```yaml
Campaign Name: Coffee Shop Loyalty Program
Token Symbol: COFFEE
Total Supply: 10000
Tokens per Claim: 10
Max Claims per Wallet: 5
Blockchain: Solana
Expiration: Enabled (December 31, 2024)
```

### YouTube Subscriber Rewards
```yaml
Campaign Name: YouTube Subscriber Rewards
Token Symbol: YT
Total Supply: 5000
Tokens per Claim: 5
Max Claims per Wallet: 3
Blockchain: Base
Expiration: Disabled
```

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Campaign creation is taking too long">
    Blockchain deployment can take 30-60 seconds depending on network congestion. If it takes longer than 2 minutes, check your wallet connection and try again.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Token symbol already exists">
    Each token symbol must be unique within your account. Choose a different symbol or add numbers/letters to make it unique (e.g., COFFEE2).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Image upload fails">
    Ensure your image is under 5MB and in a supported format (PNG, JPG, GIF). Try compressing the image if it's too large.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
