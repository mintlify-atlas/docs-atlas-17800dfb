## Why Stampchain?

From QR code generation to wallet integration, Stampchain provides all the tools you need to launch successful loyalty campaigns.

### Built for everyone

Whether you're a local coffee shop or a global brand, Stampchain adapts to your unique loyalty needs:

- **Local businesses** - Replace punch cards with digital rewards
- **Content creators** - Reward your most engaged fans with exclusive tokens
- **DAOs & communities** - Distribute governance tokens and POAP-style rewards
- **E-commerce brands** - Integrate with Shopify to automatically reward customers
- **Event organizers** - Create memorable experiences with claimable tokens
- **Developers & agencies** - Offer branded loyalty solutions to your clients
