# Get started with Stampchain

This guide will walk you through creating your first loyalty campaign, from account setup to distributing your first tokens.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to the Stampchain platform and sign in with your preferred wallet provider.

    <Note>
      Stampchain supports Phantom, WalletConnect, and other popular wallet providers.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access the admin dashboard">
    Once logged in, navigate to `/admin` to access your dashboard. Here you'll see:

    - **Campaigns tab** - View and manage all your loyalty campaigns
    - **Analytics tab** - Track claims and monitor campaign performance
    - **Settings tab** - Configure your account preferences

    The dashboard displays key metrics including total campaigns, total claims, and active campaigns.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create your first campaign">
    Click the "Create Campaign" button to start building your loyalty program.

    ### Campaign details

    Fill out the basic information about your campaign:

    ```typescript
    {
      name: "Coffee Shop Loyalty Program",
      tokenSymbol: "COFFEE",
      description: "Earn COFFEE tokens with every purchase",
      blockchain: "solana" // or "ethereum"
    }
    ```

    <Info>
      Token symbols are limited to 10 characters and will be automatically converted to uppercase.
    </Info>

    ### Token configuration

    Set up your token economics:

    - **Total supply** - Maximum number of tokens that will ever exist
    - **Tokens per claim** - How many tokens users receive per QR code scan
    - **Max claims per wallet** - Limit how many times each wallet can claim
    - **Token image** - Upload an image to represent your token (required)
    - **Decimals** - Token precision (defaults to 9)

    Example configuration:

    ```typescript
    {
      totalSupply: "10000",
      tokensPerClaim: "10",
      maxClaimsPerWallet: "5",
      decimals: "9"
    }
    ```

    ### Optional settings

    - **Expiration date** - Set a campaign end date
    - **Metadata URI** - Add custom metadata for your token

    <Warning>
      Once a campaign is created, certain settings like total supply and blockchain cannot be changed.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review and deploy">
    Before deploying, review your campaign in the preview panel:

    - **Total supply** - Verify the maximum token supply
    - **Tokens per claim** - Confirm the reward amount
    - **Campaign duration** - Check if expiration is set
    - **Blockchain** - Ensure you selected the correct network

    The campaign preview shows:

    ```typescript
    Campaign Preview {
      totalSupply: "10000 COFFEE",
      tokensPerClaim: "10 per claim",
      duration: "No Expiry",
      blockchain: "solana"
    }
    ```

    Click "Create Campaign" to deploy your loyalty token to the blockchain.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate QR codes">
    After your campaign is created, navigate to `/qr-generator` to create unique QR codes.

    Each QR code:
    - Is linked to your campaign
    - Contains a unique identifier for one-time use
    - Can be printed on receipts, packaging, or marketing materials
    - Expires based on your campaign settings

    <Tip>
      Generate QR codes in batches for events or bulk distribution to save time.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Distribute and track">
    Share your QR codes with customers through:

    - **Physical receipts** - Print QR codes on purchase receipts
    - **Marketing materials** - Include in flyers, posters, or packaging
    - **Digital channels** - Share via email, social media, or your website
    - **Events** - Display QR codes at conferences and meetups

    ### Customer claiming flow

    When customers scan your QR code:

    1. They're directed to `/claim` with a unique token parameter
    2. They connect their wallet (Phantom, WalletConnect, etc.)
    3. The system validates:
       - QR code hasn't been used before
       - Wallet hasn't exceeded claim limits
       - Campaign is still active
    4. Tokens are instantly transferred to their wallet

    The claim validation follows this schema:

    ```typescript
    {
      tokenId: string,
      walletAddress: string,
      wallet: "solana",
      uniqueId: string
    }
    ```

    <Note>
      Built-in fraud prevention protects against double claims, wallet limits, and QR code abuse.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor your campaign">
    Return to your admin dashboard to track performance:

    - View real-time claim statistics
    - Monitor remaining token supply
    - Analyze user engagement patterns
    - Download campaign reports

    The analytics dashboard provides insights into:
    - Total claims over time
    - Unique wallets claiming tokens
    - Popular claim times and locations
    - Campaign ROI metrics
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Need help?

If you encounter any issues during setup:

- Check that your wallet is connected and on the correct network
- Ensure you have sufficient funds for transaction fees
- Verify all required fields are filled correctly
- Review campaign settings before deployment

<Warning>
  Campaign settings like total supply and blockchain cannot be changed after creation. Double-check all parameters before deploying.
</Warning>
